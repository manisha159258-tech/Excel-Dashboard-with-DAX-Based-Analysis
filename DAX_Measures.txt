# DAX Measures for Excel Dashboard

1. Avg Order Value (K)
Formula:
= [Total Order]
Example: Salomon Shoes → 25.4M
Description: Average order value in thousands.

2. Total Order
Formula:
= [Top Product]
Example: Salomon Shoes → 25.4M
Description: Total sales for the top product.

3. Top Product
Formula:
= CONCATENATEX(
    TOPN(
        1,
        SUMMARIZE(Table1, Table1[Product], "total", SUM(Table1[Sales])),
        [total],
        DESC
    ),
    Table1[Product] & "-" & FORMAT([total]/1000000,"0.0") & "M"
)
Example: Salomon Shoes → 25.4M
Description: Top-selling product with total sales in millions.

4. Top Region
Formula:
= CONCATENATEX(
    TOPN(
        1,
        SUMMARIZE(Table1, Table1[Region], "total", SUM(Table1[Sales])),
        [total],
        DESC
    ),
    Table1[Region] & "-" & FORMAT([total]/1000000,"0.0") & "M"
)
Example: West → 45.7M
Description: Region with highest total sales in millions.

5. YoY Growth %
Formula:
VAR LatestYear = YEAR(MAX(Table1[Date]))
VAR CurrentSales = CALCULATE([Total Sale], FILTER(ALL(Table1), YEAR(Table1[Date]) = LatestYear))
VAR LastYearSales = CALCULATE([Total Sale], FILTER(ALL(Table1), YEAR(Table1[Date]) = LatestYear - 1))
RETURN DIVIDE(CurrentSales - LastYearSales, LastYearSales) * 100
Example: 87.59%
Description: Year-over-year sales growth percentage.

6. Total Outlet Sales
Formula:
= CALCULATE(COUNTROWS('Table1'), 'Table1'[Sales Method] = "Outlet")
Example: 3010
Description: Counts sales made via outlet method.

7. Unit Price Average
Formula:
= AVERAGE([Unit Price])
Example: 45.22
Description: Average unit price of products.

8. Total Quantity
Formula:
= SUM([Qty])
Example: 2,803,643
Description: Total units sold.

9. Average Quantity
Formula:
= AVERAGE([Qty])
Example: 290.86
Description: Average units sold per order.

10. Total Sale (M)
Formula:
= FORMAT(SUM([Sales])/1000000, "0.0") & "M"
Example: 131.6M
Description: Total sales in millions.

11. Average Sale
Formula:
= FORMAT(AVERAGE([Sales])/1000, "0.0") & "K"
Example: 13.7K
Description: Average sale per transaction in thousands.

12. Total Sale
Formula:
= SUM([Sales])
Example: 131,627,891
Description: Total sales in raw currency.
